How we work

Our core values

Reliability

Agreements are agreements. What we commit to, we deliver. You can count on consistent execution and transparent communication — even when things get complex.

Flexibility

Your volumes fluctuate. Your requirements change. We adapt — without you needing to sign a new contract or renegotiate every time.

Precision

Operational errors cost money and trust. We work with fixed quality controls, clear instructions and experienced teams who know what's at stake.

Speed

From request to execution, we move fast. We are used to working under time pressure and respond immediately — even in urgent or unexpected situations.

Corporate social responsibility

Business with impact

Good business goes beyond quality and delivery reliability. We take our responsibility toward people, the environment and society — concretely and demonstrably.

Social enterprise

We provide workplaces for people with distance to the labour market — through reintegration programmes, partnerships with sheltered workshops and home workers. Partner of MetWerk (since 2020). SBB-accredited learning company.

Fair trade

We operate in accordance with the ETI Base Code, based on the conventions of the International Labour Organization (ILO). Transparency in our chain is ensured through SEDEX — an internationally recognised platform for ethical supply chains.

Environment & energy

We actively take measures to reduce our ecological footprint: energy savings in our facilities, consistent waste separation and recycling of materials on the shop floor.
