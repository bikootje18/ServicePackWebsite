What we do

Our services

From fulfillment to rework — Service Pack provides operational support that can be deployed immediately and tailored to your processes.

Fulfillment

Your orders processed on time and accurately. Service Pack manages your full fulfillment operation — from inbound and storage to picking, packing and shipping. We work with your systems or our own, and scale with your volume whether you process 500 or 50,000 orders per week.

Inbound and put-away Inventory management Tailored pick & pack Shipping Returns processing

Warehousing

Flexible storage without long-term commitments. Our warehouse facilities support both short-term and structural storage, combined with active inventory management. Real-time stock visibility without running your own warehouse.

Bulk and location storage Real-time stock visibility On-demand inbound and outbound Combined with fulfillment

Labeling

Labels must be right, every time. We handle professional labeling of products, pallets, parcels and packaging according to your specifications or retail requirements. From EAN barcodes to multilingual market labels, all with batch-level quality control.

Productlabeling (EAN, FNSKU, CE) Palletlabeling Multilingual labels Batch quality control

Rework

When a product does not meet requirements, Service Pack fixes it. We perform rework on products, packaging or kits quickly, in a controlled and traceable way, from simple corrections to complex reconfiguration projects.

Production deviation correction Part replacement Retesting and re-inspection Documentation and reporting

Assembly

From loose components to finished products or retail-ready kits. Service Pack handles assembly based on your instructions, with built-in quality checks throughout the process. We assemble exactly what your customer expects, down to the last detail.

Product assembly & kitting Gift sets and bundles Insert manuals and leaflets Serialization and registration

Repacking

New market, new retailer or updated specifications? We repack your products efficiently, fully compliant and without quality loss. Whether it is seasonal packaging, retail specs or new market requirements.

Repacking for new markets Retail specifications Seasonal and promo packaging Quality control

Hub services

Service Pack operates as an operational hub for cross-docking, consolidation and distribution. Ideal for businesses that need a smart intermediate point in their supply chain, combined with value-added services on site.

Cross-docking and flow-through Shipment consolidation Distribution to DCs Integrated with fulfillment
