What we do

Containers unloaded quickly and correctly

Service Pack unloads containers daily for importers, manufacturers and distributors. Our location 30 minutes from the ports of Rotterdam and Antwerp makes us a logical link between port and final destination.

We combine fast unloading with immediate intake registration, quality control and connection to follow-up processes such as labeling, repacking or storage. You receive a complete unloading report per container.

Manual unloading
Hand unloading for mixed containers or fragile goods.
Mechanical unloading
Fork lift unloading for palletised cargo.
Counting and registration
Item-by-item or pallet-by-pallet counting with documentation.
Quality control
Visual inspection of goods upon receipt — deviations reported immediately.
Direct warehouse intake
Goods directly into our warehouse system after unloading.
Connection to follow-up processes
Labeling, repacking or further distribution directly after unloading.
