What we do

Assembly done right, every time

Service Pack assembles products, kits, displays and gift sets for retailers, e-commerce players and manufacturers. We build and fill over 1,000 displays monthly for clients in retail and FMCG — with consistent quality and on-time delivery.

Our assembly teams work on the basis of your work instructions, with fixed inspection points in the process. You receive regular progress reports and full documentation upon completion.
