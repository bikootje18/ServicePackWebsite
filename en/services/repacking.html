What we do

Repacked, compliant and on time

Service Pack repacks products for manufacturers, importers and retail brands. Whether it concerns new market requirements, updated retail specifications or seasonal editions — we execute efficiently and with quality control throughout.

Our repacking teams are experienced in high-volume assignments under time pressure. We provide documentation of quantities, deviations and actions taken.

Opening and repacking
Carefully opening existing packaging without product damage.
New packaging application
Applying new boxes, sleeves, bags or foil.
Retail specifications
Packaging to exact retailer requirements (barcode placement, dimensions).
Seasonal packaging

Labeling included
